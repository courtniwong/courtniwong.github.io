Visualizations by the Kettle Chips

Before creating our visualizations, we had to filter out some information
from the original San Francisco Budget dataset.
For more information, click here.

Stacked Bar Chart

This visualizes the total spending per fiscal year as well as the total spending per organization group per fiscal year. It is evident that the total spending per fiscal year has been increasing since 2010.
Created by Ray Wang

Heat Map

Heat map showing revenue generated each year by each department. The department of Public Heath consistently generates the most revenue amongst all departments!
Created by Courtni Wong

Stacked Area Chart

This visualizes the total spending per year for each department. Interesting Fact: The government spend most money on operating.
Created by Jim Gong

Multi-Series Line Chart

This visualizes the revenue generated by each department per year. Most of the revenue comes from the Operating section within the Funding Category
Created by Amanda Cieslak

  • More Info
More information on data filtered from the original dataset.
Mouse over each item for an in depth description.
The only columns we used were:
  • Fiscal Year
  • Fund Category
  • Organizational Group
  • Department
  • Amount
  • Revenue or Spending
A total of 6 columns and 110,088 rows of data were used.
All revenue and spending are adjusted for inflation using an inflation calculator and reflected in our visualizations.

Created by Ray Wang and Courtni Wong
